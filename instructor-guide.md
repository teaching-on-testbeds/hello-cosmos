## Guide for instructors

If you are using COSMOS in the classroom, complete these steps before you onboard your students:

1. **Register a COSMOS group for your course.** COSMOS calls its user organizations "groups" rather than projects. If you are a faculty or staff member, or a project manager at an eligible institution, submit the [organization registration form](https://www.cosmos-lab.org/portal/register-org). Use an institutional email address that COSMOS staff can verify.
2. **Confirm that your group appears on the user registration form.** Check the list of organizations on the [user registration form](https://www.cosmos-lab.org/register_usr).
3. **Approve account requests.** The person who registers the group becomes its PI. The PI approves account requests from students and course staff.

Once you have completed these steps, ask your students to create accounts by following [Hello, COSMOS: Create an account](https://teaching-on-testbeds.github.io/hello-cosmos/create-account).

* Tell them the group name they should select on the registration form.
* Students must confirm their request by email before the PI can approve it. Ask them to register in advance of their first anticipated use of COSMOS.
